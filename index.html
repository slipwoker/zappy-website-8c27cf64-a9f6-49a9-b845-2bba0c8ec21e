Broken Spring Repair

That loud bang from your garage is almost always a snapped torsion or extension spring. When your door won't lift — or lifts only a few inches — a broken spring is the culprit. We carry springs for every door size and replace them same day.

Learn about spring repair →

Opener Repair & Programming

Remote not responding? Motor running but door not moving? Grinding or clicking sounds from the unit? We diagnose and repair all major opener brands — LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Genie, and more — and reprogram remotes and keypads on the spot.

Learn about opener repair →

Off-Track & Cable Repair

A door that looks crooked, scrapes one side, or has a visibly slack cable is off-track or has a snapped lift cable. Operating it risks serious damage. We realign rollers, straighten bent tracks, and replace frayed or broken cables to restore safe operation.
